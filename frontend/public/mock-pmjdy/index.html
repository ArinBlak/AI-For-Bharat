About The Scheme

What is PMJDY?

Launched on 28th August 2014, it is one of the world's largest financial inclusion initiatives.

💳

Zero Balance Account

No minimum balance required for opening a Basic Savings Bank Deposit (BSBD) account under PMJDY.

🛡️

Insurance Cover

Accidental Insurance Cover of ₹2 Lakh with RuPay card issued to new PMJDY account holders.

💸

Overdraft Facility

Overdraft facility up to ₹10,000 available to eligible account holders after 6 months of operation.

📈

Direct Benefit Transfer

Beneficiaries of Government Schemes will get Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) in these accounts.

Key Pillars

Six Pillars of PMJDY

1

Universal Access

Providing banking services to every unbanked household through banking outlets and business correspondents.

2

Basic Banking Accounts

Accounts with RuPay Debit card and accident insurance cover of ₹2 lakh.

3

Financial Literacy

Promoting savings and use of ATM/Mobile Banking through financial literacy programmes.
